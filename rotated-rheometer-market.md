<p><strong>The Rotated Rheometer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Stress Controlled Type Rotated Rheometer</li><li>Strain Controlled Type Rotated Rheometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rotated rheometer market is divided into two main types: stress-controlled and strain-controlled rheometers. Stress-controlled rheometers apply a controlled stress to the sample and measure the resulting strain, making them ideal for materials with variable viscosities. In contrast, strain-controlled rheometers apply a controlled strain and measure the resulting stress, suitable for studying materials that exhibit time-dependent behavior. Both types are essential for characterizing the flow properties of liquids and soft solids across various industries, including pharmaceuticals, food, and polymers.</p></p>
