<p><p>Tetraethyl Orthosilicate (TEOS) is a key chemical used in industries like coatings, electronics, and ceramics. The **Direct Method** involves the direct reaction of ethanol with silicon tetrachloride, producing TEOS with high purity, suitable for advanced applications. The **STC Method** (Silicon Tetrachloride Method) uses silicon tetrachloride as a raw material, reacting with ethanol, offering cost efficiency and scalability for bulk production. Both methods cater to diverse market needs, with the Direct Method focusing on precision and the STC Method emphasizing large-scale industrial use.</p></p>
