<p><p>The sleeve labeling market encompasses various types, primarily including heat-shrink and stretch sleeve labels. Heat-shrink sleeves contract when heated, conforming tightly to the container, often used for branding and product protection. Stretch sleeve labels, made from flexible materials, are applied by stretching them over the product, ensuring a secure fit without heat. Additionally, the market includes other specialized labeling options like tamper-evident and decorative labels, catering to diverse packaging needs across industries such as food, beverage, and personal care.</p></p>
