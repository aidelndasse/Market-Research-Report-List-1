<p><p>The spin casting fishing rods market can be divided into two main types: freshwater spinning rods and saltwater spinning rods. Freshwater spinning rods are designed for use in lakes, rivers, and streams, offering lighter construction and sensitivity for targeting species like trout and bass. In contrast, saltwater spinning rods are built to withstand harsher conditions and are typically more robust, designed for fishing in oceans and seas where larger and harder-fighting species like tuna and marlin are targeted.</p></p>
