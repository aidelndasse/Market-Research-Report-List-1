<p><p>The party balloon market can be divided into two primary types: latex party balloons and foil party balloons. Latex party balloons are made from natural rubber and are biodegradable, making them eco-friendly options for events. They are available in various colors and sizes, and they can be inflated with air or helium. Foil party balloons, on the other hand, are made from mylar and are known for their durability and ability to hold helium for longer periods. They often feature printed designs and shapes, making them popular for themed celebrations.</p></p>
