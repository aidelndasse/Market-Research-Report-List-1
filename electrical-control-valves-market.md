<p><ul><li>Ball Valves</li><li>Butterfly Valves</li><li>Gate Valves</li><li>Plug Valves</li><li>Piston Valves</li><li>Diaphragm Valves</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electrical control valves market includes several types, each serving specific applications. Ball valves offer quick shut-off with low resistance and are ideal for on/off control. Butterfly valves are compact and lightweight, suitable for regulating flow in large pipes. Gate valves provide a straight-through unobstructed flow, best for isolation. Plug valves enable versatile flow control with minimal pressure loss. Piston valves are used for precise dosing and pressure control, while diaphragm valves allow for sanitary applications and are excellent for corrosive fluids.</p></p>
