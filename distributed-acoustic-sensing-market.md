<p><strong>The Distributed Acoustic Sensing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Longitudinal Wave Acoustic Sensing</li><li>Transverse Wave Acoustic Sensing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Distributed Acoustic Sensing (DAS) market is primarily categorized into two types: Longitudinal Wave Acoustic Sensing and Transverse Wave Acoustic Sensing. Longitudinal wave sensing detects pressure changes along the fiber, making it effective for monitoring events like seismic activities and pipeline conditions. Transverse wave sensing, on the other hand, measures shear waves, which are useful for applications involving vibration analysis and structural health monitoring. Both types leverage fiber optic technology for real-time, high-resolution data collection across extensive areas.</p></p>
