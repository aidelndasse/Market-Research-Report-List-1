<p><ul><li>Single Acting Steam Valve</li><li>Double Acting Steam Valve</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electric steam valves market primarily consists of single acting and double acting steam valves, each serving distinct applications. Single acting steam valves typically require a spring return mechanism, allowing for quicker response times in on-off control, ideal for less complex systems. In contrast, double acting steam valves utilize electrical power for both opening and closing, providing enhanced control and precision, making them suitable for more demanding applications where consistent flow regulation is essential. Both types are critical in industrial processes.</p></p>
