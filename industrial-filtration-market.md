<p><p>The industrial filtration market encompasses two primary segments: liquid filtration and air filtration. Liquid filtration involves removing contaminants from fluids, utilized in various industries like water treatment, food processing, and chemical manufacturing. Techniques may include membrane, cartridge, or bag filtration. Conversely, air filtration focuses on purifying the air in environments such as HVAC systems, manufacturing plants, and clean rooms, employing filters like HEPA, activated carbon, or electrostatic filters to capture dust, allergens, and other airborne pollutants. Both markets are essential for maintaining operational efficiency and environmental compliance.</p></p>
