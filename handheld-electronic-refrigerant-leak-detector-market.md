<p><strong>The Handheld Electronic Refrigerant Leak Detector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Infrared</li><li>Semiconductor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The handheld electronic refrigerant leak detector market primarily comprises two types: infrared and semiconductor detectors. Infrared detectors utilize infrared sensors to identify refrigerant leaks based on the absorption of specific wavelengths, offering high sensitivity and the ability to distinguish between different gases. Semiconductor detectors, on the other hand, rely on changes in conductivity when gas comes into contact with a semiconductor material, providing a cost-effective solution for leak detection, although they may have a higher false positive rate compared to infrared devices.</p></p>
