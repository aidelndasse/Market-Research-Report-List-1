<p><p>The glass-free UHD 3D displays market encompasses two primary technologies: parallax barrier and lenticular display. Parallax barrier technology utilizes a series of slits to direct light, allowing each eye to see different images for a 3D effect, making it suitable for handheld devices. In contrast, lenticular display technology employs an array of lenses to create depth by projecting multiple views simultaneously, ideal for larger screens. Both technologies aim to enhance viewer experience by delivering immersive, high-definition 3D visuals without the need for glasses.</p></p>
