<p><strong>The Healthcare Simulation Software Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Spreadsheets</li><li>Discrete Event Simulators</li><li>Agent-based Simulators</li><li>Continuous Simulators</li><li>Hybrid Simulators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Healthcare simulation software encompasses various market types, each serving distinct needs. Spreadsheets are simple tools for data management and analysis. Discrete Event Simulators model events that occur at specific times, capturing patient flow and resource allocation. Agent-based Simulators focus on individual entities' behaviors and interactions within the healthcare system. Continuous Simulators utilize differential equations for systems that change fluidly over time. Hybrid Simulators combine elements of the previous types to provide a more comprehensive modeling approach for complex healthcare scenarios.</p></p>
