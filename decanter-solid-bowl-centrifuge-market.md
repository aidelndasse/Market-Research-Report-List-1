Concurrent design involves the simultaneous movement of the feed and solids in the same direction, optimizing separation efficiency. In contrast, Counter Current design allows the feed and solids to move in opposite directions, enhancing the recovery of valuable materials. Each design offers unique advantages in specific applications, catering to various industry needs for effective solid-liquid separation processes.</p></p>
