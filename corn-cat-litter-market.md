<p><ul><li>Reunion</li><li>Granular</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The corn cat litter market is primarily divided into two types: reunion and granular. Reunion cat litter is characterized by its clumping ability, which makes it easy to remove waste while minimizing odors. This type is often favored for its natural composition and biodegradable properties. On the other hand, granular cat litter consists of small, non-clumping particles that absorb moisture effectively but may require more frequent changes. Both types cater to different user preferences and environmental considerations in cat care.</p></p>
