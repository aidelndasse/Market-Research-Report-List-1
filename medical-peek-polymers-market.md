<p><p>The Medical PEEK (Polyether Ether Ketone) polymers market is segmented into two main types: powder and particle. PEEK powder is a fine particulate form utilized in additive manufacturing and injection molding to create precise medical components, such as implants and surgical instruments. In contrast, PEEK particles consist of larger granules, often used in processes like compression molding. Both forms offer excellent biocompatibility, mechanical strength, and chemical resistance, making them ideal for various medical applications, especially in orthopedic and dental fields.</p></p>
