Class 1 gloves are rated for voltages up to 7,500 volts, suitable for low-voltage applications. Class 2 gloves offer protection for up to 17,000 volts, ideal for medium-voltage environments. Class 3 and Class 4 gloves provide higher protection, rated for up to 26,500 volts and 36,000 volts, respectively, making them essential for high-voltage power distribution and maintenance tasks. Each class ensures safety for workers in electrical fields.</p></p>
