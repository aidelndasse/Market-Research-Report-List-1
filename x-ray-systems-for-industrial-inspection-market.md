<p><strong>The X-ray Systems for Industrial Inspection Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Digital Radiography (DR) Type</li><li>Computed Tomography (CT) Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>X-ray systems for industrial inspection are primarily categorized into Digital Radiography (DR) and Computed Tomography (CT). DR utilizes digital detectors to capture images quickly and efficiently, allowing for real-time analysis of materials and structures, which enhances workflow and reduces inspection time. CT, on the other hand, uses a series of X-ray images taken at various angles to create cross-sectional views of objects, providing a detailed 3D representation. Both types are essential for ensuring quality and safety in various industries.</p></p>
