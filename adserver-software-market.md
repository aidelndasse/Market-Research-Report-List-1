<p><ul><li>Cloud-Based</li><li>On-Premise</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Adserver software is categorized into two main market types: cloud-based and on-premise. Cloud-based ad servers are hosted on remote servers and accessed via the internet, offering scalability, ease of use, and lower upfront costs, making them ideal for businesses looking for flexibility and reduced maintenance. In contrast, on-premise ad servers are installed locally on a company's servers, providing greater control, data security, and customization, but often requiring significant investment in hardware and ongoing maintenance.</p></p>
