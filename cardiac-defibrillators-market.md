<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Cardiac Defibrillators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Automatic External Defibrillators</li><li>Implantable Cardioverter Defibrillators</li><li>Wearable Defibrillators</li><li>Advanced Life Support (ALS) Defibrillators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cardiac defibrillator market consists of several key types: </p><p>1. **Automatic External Defibrillators (AEDs)** are portable devices designed for laypersons to use in emergencies, analyzing heart rhythms and delivering shocks if necessary.</p><p>   </p><p>2. **Implantable Cardioverter Defibrillators (ICDs)** are surgically implanted devices that monitor heart activity and deliver shocks automatically to correct dangerous arrhythmias.</p><p>   </p><p>3. **Wearable Defibrillators** are external devices worn by patients at risk for sudden cardiac arrest, providing shocks when abnormal heart rhythms are detected.</p><p>4. **Advanced Life Support (ALS) Defibrillators** are sophisticated devices used by emergency medical professionals, featuring advanced monitoring capabilities and manual settings for critical care situations.</p></p>
