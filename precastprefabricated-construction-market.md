<p><ul><li>Modular Built</li><li>Panel Built</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The precast and prefabricated construction market encompasses various types, primarily categorized into modular built and panel built systems. Modular built construction involves assembling entire sections or units offsite, which are then transported and installed on-site, promoting efficiency and reduced construction time. In contrast, panel built systems focus on manufacturing large wall panels or structural components offsite, which are later assembled at the construction site. Both methods enhance quality control, reduce waste, and streamline the building process.</p></p>
