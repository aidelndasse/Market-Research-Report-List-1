<p><strong>The Direct-driven Wind Turbine Generator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Type I</li><li>Type II</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The direct-driven wind turbine generator market is categorized into Type I and Type II based on their design and operational features. Type I generators utilize a traditional synchronous generator connected to a gearbox, offering robustness but with increased maintenance requirements. In contrast, Type II generators feature a permanent magnet generator that operates without a gearbox, enhancing efficiency and reducing maintenance. Both types cater to varying wind conditions and energy requirements, influencing their adoption in different regions and applications within the renewable energy sector.</p></p>
