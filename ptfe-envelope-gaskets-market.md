Type A gaskets feature a solid PTFE core with a non-asbestos material for enhanced flexibility. Type B gaskets have a PTFE envelope with a supporting structure, providing added strength and durability. Type C gaskets are designed with a PTFE covering that offers excellent chemical resistance, making them suitable for aggressive environments. Each type serves distinct applications, ensuring efficient sealing solutions across industries.</p></p>
