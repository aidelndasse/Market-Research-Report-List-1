<p><p>The automotive electric oil pump market is primarily categorized into two types: separate pumps and integrated pumps. Separate pumps function independently to circulate oil within the engine, offering flexibility in design and installation. In contrast, integrated pumps are designed as part of the engine system, enhancing compactness and efficiency. Both types play crucial roles in improving lubrication, reducing friction, and enhancing overall engine performance. Their selection depends on vehicle requirements and design preferences.</p></p>
