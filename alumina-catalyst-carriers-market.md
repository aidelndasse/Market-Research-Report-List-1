<p><p>The alumina catalyst carriers market primarily consists of two types: alumina microsphere and alumina powder. Alumina microspheres are spherical alumina particles known for their high surface area, uniformity, and low density, making them ideal for use in catalytic processes and refining applications. Alumina powder, on the other hand, consists of fine alumina particles that offer versatility in various catalytic applications due to their stability and ability to support active catalyst materials. Both types enhance catalyst performance in diverse industrial applications.</p></p>
