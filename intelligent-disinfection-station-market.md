<p><p>The Intelligent Disinfection Station market is categorized into two primary types: dripping and spray systems. Dripping systems deliver disinfectant in controlled droplets, ensuring precise application on surfaces, which minimizes waste and enhances effectiveness. In contrast, spray systems utilize aerosolization to distribute disinfectant evenly over a wide area, allowing for rapid coverage of larger surfaces. Both methods aim to enhance hygiene and safety across various environments, including healthcare facilities, public spaces, and commercial establishments, adapting to diverse disinfection needs.</p></p>
