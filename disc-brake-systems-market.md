<p><p>The disc brake systems market consists of various types, primarily focusing on piston type and floating caliper type designs. Piston type disc brakes utilize fixed pistons to clamp the brake pads against the rotor, providing consistent braking power, ideal for high-performance vehicles. In contrast, floating caliper types feature a movable caliper that shifts to apply pressure on the pads, offering a lightweight solution with effective braking. Both types cater to diverse automotive applications, enhancing vehicle safety and performance.</p></p>
