<p><ul><li>Self-Expandable Metal Stents</li><li>Nitinol Stent</li><li>Elgiloy</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The colonic stents market features three main types: self-expandable metal stents, Nitinol stents, and Elgiloy stents. Self-expandable metal stents are designed to expand automatically upon placement, providing immediate support to obstructed colon areas. Nitinol stents, made from a nickel-titanium alloy, offer superior flexibility and strength, adapting well to the complex anatomy of the colon. Elgiloy stents, comprised of a cobalt-chromium alloy, are known for their excellent corrosion resistance and durability, making them suitable for long-term applications in the gastrointestinal tract.</p></p>
