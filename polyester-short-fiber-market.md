<p><p>The polyester short fiber market can be divided into two main types: solid and hollow fibers. Solid polyester fibers are densely packed, offering strength and durability, making them suitable for a wide range of applications, including textiles and non-woven products. In contrast, hollow polyester fibers feature a void or hollow core, which enhances insulation and softness, making them ideal for applications like padding and insulation in garments and home furnishings. Both types cater to diverse consumer needs across various industries.</p></p>
