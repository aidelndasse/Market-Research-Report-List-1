<p><ul><li>Near Infrared Resonance (NIR)</li><li>Radio Frequency (RF)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The online moisture analyzers market includes Near Infrared Resonance (NIR) and Radio Frequency (RF) technologies. NIR analyzers utilize light absorption properties of materials to determine moisture content, offering quick, non-destructive measurements ideal for various industries. RF analyzers, on the other hand, measure moisture based on the dielectric properties of materials through electromagnetic waves, providing accurate readings in bulk materials. Both technologies enhance process control, improve product quality, and reduce waste in applications like food, pharmaceuticals, and agriculture.</p></p>
