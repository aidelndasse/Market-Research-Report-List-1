<p><p>Smart water metering encompasses various market types, notably remote transmitting water meters and pre-payment electricity meters. Remote transmitting water meters efficiently gather and transmit usage data to utility providers, enhancing accuracy and reducing manual reading costs. In contrast, pre-payment electricity meters allow consumers to pay for energy in advance, encouraging budget management and reducing utility arrears. Both technologies aim to improve resource management, optimize customer engagement, and streamline operational efficiency for utility companies in the evolving energy landscape.</p></p>
