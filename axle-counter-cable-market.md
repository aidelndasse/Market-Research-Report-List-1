<p><p>The Axle Counter Cable market primarily features two types: Twisted Pair Cables and Star-Quad Cables. Twisted Pair Cables consist of pairs of insulated wires twisted together, minimizing electromagnetic interference, making them ideal for communication lines in railway applications. Star-Quad Cables feature four insulated wires arranged in a star formation, enhancing noise immunity and signal quality. Both types are crucial for axle counting systems, ensuring accurate train tracking and operational efficiency while maintaining safety within railway networks.</p></p>
