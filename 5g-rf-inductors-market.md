<p><p>The 5G RF inductors market comprises several types, each catering to specific applications. Wire wound inductors, constructed with a wire coiled around a core, offer high inductance values and are ideal for power applications. Film type inductors, made from thin films, provide greater precision and are suitable for high-frequency use. Multilayer inductors, featuring multiple layers of conductive materials, excel in space-constrained environments, enabling compact designs with enhanced performance. Together, these types support the growing demands of 5G technology.</p></p>
