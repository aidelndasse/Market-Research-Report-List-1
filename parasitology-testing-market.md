<p><p>The Parasitology Testing Market encompasses various techniques for detecting and analyzing parasites. Microscopic Identification involves examining samples under a microscope to spot parasites directly. Molecular Techniques utilize methods like PCR to identify genetic material of parasites, offering high specificity. Immunological Techniques rely on antigen-antibody interactions to detect parasites or their metabolites. Rapid Diagnostic Tests (RDTs) provide quick results using simple procedures, often for specific infections. Each method caters to different diagnostic needs and enhances the overall understanding of parasitic diseases.</p></p>
