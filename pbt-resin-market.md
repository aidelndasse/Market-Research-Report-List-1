<p><p>The PBT resin market is divided into two main types: injection grade and extrusion grade PBT. Injection grade PBT is designed for use in injection molding applications, offering high strength, thermal stability, and good surface finish, making it suitable for complex components in automotive and electrical industries. Extrusion grade PBT, on the other hand, is tailored for extrusion processes, providing excellent flow and mechanical properties, ideal for creating profiles, sheets, and other continuous shapes in various industrial applications.</p></p>
