<p><p>The Clinical Diagnostics Mass Spectrometer market primarily includes three types: LC-MS (Liquid Chromatography-Mass Spectrometry), GC-MS (Gas Chromatography-Mass Spectrometry), and others. LC-MS is widely used for analyzing biomolecules in complex biological samples, offering high sensitivity and specificity. GC-MS is effective for volatile and semi-volatile compounds, aiding in the analysis of metabolites and environmental samples. Other types may include techniques like MALDI-TOF and ICP-MS, catering to diverse applications such as proteomics, toxicology, and therapeutic drug monitoring.</p></p>
