Iron-based end mills offer a balance of hardness and toughness, making them suitable for general machining applications. Nickel-based end mills provide superior heat resistance, ideal for high-temperature environments. Cobalt-based end mills combine strength and wear resistance, enhancing the performance of cutting tools in demanding machining processes. Each type serves distinct industrial needs, influencing market dynamics.</p></p>
