<p><strong>The Metal O-Rings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Internal Pressure Non-vented</li><li>External Pressure Vented</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Metal O-Rings market includes two primary types: Internal Pressure Non-Vented and External Pressure Vented. Internal Pressure Non-Vented O-Rings are designed to maintain sealing integrity under positive internal pressure, making them ideal for applications like hydraulic systems. In contrast, External Pressure Vented O-Rings manage external pressure by allowing airflow, preventing vacuum conditions that could compromise seals. Both types serve critical roles in industries requiring reliable sealing solutions, ensuring performance in varying pressure environments.</p></p>
