<p><p>Contact heart mapping encompasses various market types focused on visualizing cardiac electrical activity. Electroanatomical Mapping integrates 3D imaging with electrophysiological data for precise arrhythmia localization. Basket Catheter Mapping utilizes multi-electrode catheters to simultaneously assess large areas of the heart, enhancing diagnostic efficiency. Traditional Endocardial Catheter Mapping involves direct contact with the heart tissue to record electrical signals, typically requiring greater effort and time. Each method offers unique advantages for diagnosing and treating cardiac conditions.</p></p>
