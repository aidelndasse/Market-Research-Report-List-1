<p><p>PVC Waterstop is a flexible, durable material made from polyvinyl chloride (PVC) used to prevent the passage of water in construction joints, particularly in concrete structures such as basements, tunnels, water treatment plants, and reservoirs. <p><ul><li>Centerbulb Type</li><li>Dumbbell Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PVC waterstop market includes various types designed for construction joints to prevent water leakage. The **Centerbulb Type** features a central bulb that compresses to create a tight seal, ideal for expansion joints and areas with movement. The **Dumbbell Type** has two bulbs on either side, providing enhanced sealing for static joints. Both types are durable, flexible, and resistant to chemicals, making them suitable for water-retaining structures like dams, tunnels, and basements, ensuring long-term waterproofing performance.</p></p>
