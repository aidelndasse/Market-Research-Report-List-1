<p><p>The video security and surveillance equipment market primarily consists of two main types: IP cameras and analog cameras. IP cameras utilize digital technology to transmit high-resolution video over networks, allowing for remote access and advanced features like motion detection and night vision. In contrast, analog cameras operate on traditional coaxial cables, delivering lower resolution footage but often at a lower cost. Both types serve distinct needs, with IP cameras favored for modern security systems and analog cameras still prevalent in many existing installations.</p></p>
