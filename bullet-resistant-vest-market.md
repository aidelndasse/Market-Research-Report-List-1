<p><p>The bullet-resistant vest market divides into two main types: soft armor and hard armor. Soft armor comprises flexible materials designed to absorb and dissipate impact from bullets, making it lightweight and suitable for everyday wear. It primarily offers protection against handgun rounds. In contrast, hard armor uses rigid plates made from materials like ceramic or polyethylene to provide greater protection against higher-caliber threats, including rifles. Each type serves distinct user needs based on threat levels and mobility requirements.</p></p>
