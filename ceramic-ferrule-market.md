<p><ul><li>SC and FC Ceramic Ferrule</li><li>ST Ceramic Ferrule</li><li>LC Ceramic Ferrule</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ceramic ferrule market includes various types, primarily SC, FC, ST, and LC ferrules. SC (Subscriber Connector) ferrules provide efficient optical fiber termination with low insertion loss, while FC (Ferrule Connector) ferrules are known for high precision and durability. ST (Straight Tip) ferrules offer quick coupling and are widely used in multi-mode applications. LC (Lucent Connector) ferrules are compact and enable high-density connections. Other types may include specialized ferrules for unique applications, enhancing market diversity.</p></p>
