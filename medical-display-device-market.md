<p><p>The medical display device market is primarily divided into two types: LED (Light Emitting Diode) and OLED (Organic Light Emitting Diode) displays. LED displays offer high brightness, energy efficiency, and durability, making them suitable for various medical imaging applications. Both technologies cater to the growing demand for advanced imaging solutions in healthcare, improving diagnosis and treatment outcomes.</p></p>
