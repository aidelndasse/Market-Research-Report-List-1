<p><p>The Dust Containment System market comprises two main types: isolate dust and remove dust systems. Isolate dust systems focus on preventing dust from spreading in work environments, often through the use of barriers or enclosures that contain dust and minimize exposure. In contrast, remove dust systems actively extract and filter airborne particles from the environment, using fans and filtration technology to maintain air quality. Both systems are essential for safeguarding health and ensuring workplace cleanliness in various industries.</p></p>
