<p><p>Valve market types include various categories based on their functions. A shut-off valve is used to stop the flow of fluid entirely, while a control valve regulates flow and pressure to ensure optimal performance. Vent valves allow gases or liquids to escape to prevent pressure buildup, while diverter valves direct fluid to different paths in a system. Other valves encompass specialized types like check, relief, and pressure-regulating valves, each serving unique roles in fluid management across industries.</p></p>
