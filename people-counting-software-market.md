<p><p>People counting software can be categorized into three main types: IR Beam, Thermal Imaging, and Video-Based systems. </p><p>IR Beam systems use infrared sensors to detect movement across a beam, providing accurate counts but limited to specific entry points. Thermal imaging detects body heat, allowing for counting in diverse environments and lighting conditions. Video-based systems utilize cameras and advanced analytics to track movements in real-time, offering comprehensive data and insights about crowd behavior, but may require more processing power and storage.</p></p>
