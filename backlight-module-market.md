<p><ul><li>CCFL Backlight Module</li><li>LED Backlight Module</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The backlight module market is primarily categorized into two types: CCFL (Cold Cathode Fluorescent Lamp) and LED (Light Emitting Diode) backlight modules. CCFL backlights, known for their vibrant colors and uniform brightness, are commonly used in older displays but are being phased out due to their bulkiness and lower energy efficiency. LED backlight modules have gained popularity for their slimmer design, improved energy efficiency, longer lifespan, and ability to provide better color accuracy, making them the preferred choice for modern displays.</p></p>
