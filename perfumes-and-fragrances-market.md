<p><p>The perfumes and fragrances market comprises various product types, each varying in concentration and longevity. Cologne typically has the lowest concentration of fragrance oils, offering a light scent ideal for casual wear. Eau de Parfum is more concentrated, providing a stronger aroma lasting several hours. Eau de Toilette is lighter than Eau de Parfum, making it suitable for daily use. Parfum, or extrait, contains the highest concentration for an intense experience. Solid perfumes are portable, wax-based formulations, perfect for on-the-go application.</p></p>
